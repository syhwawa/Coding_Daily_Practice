Write an SQL query that reports all product names of the products in the Sales table along with their selling year andÂ price.
```SQL
Sales table:
+---------+------------+------+----------+-------+
| sale_id | product_id | year | quantity | price |
+---------+------------+------+----------+-------+ 
| 1       | 100        | 2008 | 10       | 5000  |
```

# Write your MySQL query statement below
